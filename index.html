Planning

Don't let the beauty of Oregon distract you.

Do your homework before hitting the road.

Road Conditions

Wondering what the roads are doing? Well, you should be! Visit ODOT's Trip Check to check the status of your route before leaving.

Weather

It could be sunny and beautiful where you are now, but a LOT can change before you get to your final destination. Visit the National Weather Service to see what mother nature has in store for you.

Gear

The gear you carry can make or break a trip. Gear includes everything in your car, to what you may need for excursions on foot. Make sure you have the right clothes, some paper maps, a compass, plenty of water and food, an emergency vehicle kit, and more to be ready for the unexpected.
